## Notes

- Each phase can be tackled independently
- Phase 1 (Date/Luxon) is highest priority — affects correctness and timezone handling
- Phase 2-3 (loops/mutations) are the largest volume but lower risk
- Phase 4 (N+1) directly impacts production performance
- Test file Date fixes (Phase 1) are lower priority but should be cleaned up
- 3D geometry loops (Three.js vertex generation) are exempt from Phase 2
- PDFDocument stream callbacks and Puppeteer evaluate helpers are exempt from Phase 3
- URLSearchParams `.set()` calls are exempt from Phase 3
