# FieldFlow Setup Guide

This guide walks you through the initial setup process for FieldFlow, the Annix Rep field sales application.

## Prerequisites

Before starting, ensure you have:
- A valid email address for registration
- Your company information ready
- (Optional) Your calendar credentials for Google or Microsoft 365

## Step 1: Registration

1. Navigate to the FieldFlow login page
2. Click "Register" to create a new account
3. Enter your details:
   - Full name
   - Email address
   - Password (minimum 8 characters)
4. Click "Create Account"
5. Check your email for a verification link
6. Click the verification link to activate your account

## Step 2: Initial Profile Setup

After logging in for the first time, you'll be guided through the setup wizard:

### Company Information
- **Company Name**: Enter your company's legal name
- **Industry**: Select your industry from the dropdown
- **Role**: Your position (Sales Rep, Account Manager, etc.)

### Territory Configuration
- **Primary Region**: Select your main operating region (province/state)
- **Cities**: Add the cities you cover
- **Territory Name**: Give your territory a descriptive name (e.g., "Gauteng North")

### Working Hours
- **Start Time**: When your workday typically begins
- **End Time**: When your workday ends
- **Working Days**: Select the days you work (Mon-Fri by default)

## Step 3: Calendar Integration (Recommended)

Connecting your calendar enables:
- Automatic meeting sync
- Availability detection for booking links
- Conflict prevention

### Google Calendar
1. Click "Connect Google Calendar"
2. Sign in with your Google account
3. Grant FieldFlow permission to access your calendar
4. Select which calendars to sync

### Microsoft 365 / Outlook
1. Click "Connect Microsoft Calendar"
2. Sign in with your Microsoft account
3. Authorize FieldFlow access
4. Select calendars to sync

## Step 4: CRM Integration (Optional)

If you use an external CRM, you can connect it during setup:

### Supported CRMs
- Salesforce
- HubSpot
- Pipedrive

See the [CRM Integration Guide](./CRM_INTEGRATION_GUIDE.md) for detailed instructions.

## Step 5: Notification Preferences

Configure how you want to receive notifications:

- **Email Notifications**: Meeting reminders, follow-up alerts
- **Push Notifications**: Real-time alerts (requires browser permission)
- **Reminder Timing**: How far in advance to be notified (15min, 30min, 1hr)

## Completing Setup

Once you've completed all steps:
1. Review your settings
2. Click "Complete Setup"
3. You'll be redirected to your dashboard

## Troubleshooting

### Calendar not syncing?
- Ensure you've granted full calendar access
- Try disconnecting and reconnecting the calendar
- Check that the calendar isn't hidden in your calendar app

### Verification email not received?
- Check your spam/junk folder
- Request a new verification email from the login page
- Ensure the email address was entered correctly

### Setup wizard stuck?
- Refresh the page
- Clear browser cache and try again
- Use a different browser if issues persist
