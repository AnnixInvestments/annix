## 3. SADC Certificate of Origin — where it stands
- Portal: **essCert** (`esscert.idt.ice.com`, Johannesburg Chamber of Commerce & Industry). Apply → "CO – Certificate of Origin".
- Rule: **only SADC-originating goods may be listed.** Rubber sheet qualifies; the 4 chemicals need written **Supplier's Declarations of Origin** before listing.
- **Not yet started in essCert** — paused pending supplier replies. Toluene is **excluded** (imported).
