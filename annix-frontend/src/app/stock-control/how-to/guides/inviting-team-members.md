## Steps

1. Open **Settings → Team**
2. Click **Invite User**
3. Enter their email address and pick a role
4. Click **Send Invite** — they receive a sign-up link valid for 7 days
5. They click the link, set a password, and land in the portal

## Resending an invitation

If someone misses the email, open **Settings → Team → Pending** and click **Resend**. This generates a fresh link and sends a new email.

## Removing a user

From **Settings → Team**, click the row and then **Deactivate**. The user loses access immediately but their historical records remain intact.
