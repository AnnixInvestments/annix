## When to use a requisition

Raise a requisition whenever stock needs to move from one job, bin, or location to another. Requisitions create an auditable trail that bypasses the need for full delivery-note paperwork.

## Raising a requisition

1. Open **Stock → Requisitions**
2. Click **New Requisition**
3. Pick the source location and destination
4. Add the items and quantities
5. Click **Submit** — the requisition goes to the approver

## Fulfilling

When a requisition lands in your queue:

1. Physically pick the items from the source location
2. Open the requisition and tick each line as picked
3. Click **Mark Fulfilled** — stock movements are recorded
