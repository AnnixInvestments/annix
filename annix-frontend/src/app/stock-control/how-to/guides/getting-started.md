## Welcome

ASCA Stock Control is the end-to-end job tracking system for receiving goods, managing quality, and closing out jobs for customers.

## The portal layout

The top toolbar gives you access to every module you have permission to use. Your role determines which items appear:

- **Dashboard** — live view of all active jobs
- **Jobs** — job cards grouped by workflow stage
- **Quality** — QCPs, inspection points, calibration tracking
- **Stock** — locations, movements, roll stock
- **Documents** — delivery notes, invoices, CoCs
- **How To** — these guides (role-scoped)

## Switching context

If you are a manager or admin, you can use the **View As** dropdown to preview the portal from another role's perspective. This does not change your actual permissions — it only hides modules the other role cannot see.
