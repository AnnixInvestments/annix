## What branding affects

Your company branding is applied to every customer-facing PDF (delivery notes, invoices, CoCs, QCPs) and to the portal header when you are logged in.

## Steps

1. Open **Settings → Company Profile**
2. Upload your logo — square or landscape, PNG or SVG
3. Pick a primary and secondary colour
4. Fill in legal name, registration number, VAT number, and physical address
5. Upload a signature image for authorized sign-offs (optional)
6. Click **Save**

## PDF templates

Templates automatically pick up your branding. If you need custom layouts, contact Annix support — PDF templates live on the backend and cannot be edited from the portal.
