## Why stock movements matter

Every booking, requisition, and dispatch creates a stock movement record. These movements are how you answer the question "where did that item go?".

## Viewing movements

1. Open **Stock → Movements**
2. Filter by date range, location, item, or job card
3. Click any row to see the source document (delivery note, requisition, or dispatch)

## Exporting

Use the **Export** button on the top-right to download movements as CSV for analysis in Excel.
