## What this is for

Talent pools are named groups of candidates — "Available immediately", "Cape Town", "Boilermakers" — so that when a brief lands you can work from a ready group instead of searching from scratch.

## Step-by-step

1. Open **Talent Pools** and select **Create pool**.
2. Give the pool a clear name and an optional description.
3. Tick the candidates that belong in the pool.
4. Save. Open a pool any time to add or remove members.

## Rules

- Deleting a pool does not delete the candidates in it.
- Pools are shared across your agency.

## Tips

- Keep pools outcome-focused (a role family or availability), not one-offs — that's what makes them reusable.
