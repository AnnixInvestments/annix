## What this is for

When a vacancy comes in, **Matches** tells you who in your database fits — and *why* — so you can call the strongest people first.

## Step-by-step

1. Open **Matches**.
2. Enter the **required skills** (comma-separated).
3. Optionally set a **minimum years of experience** and a **preferred province**.
4. Select **Find matches**.
5. Read each result: the match %, the green ✓ skills they have, the grey ✗ skills they're missing, and any amber **concerns** (e.g. "no consent — cannot be submitted yet", "below required experience").

## Rules

- Matching runs over candidates you're allowed to see (your private candidates plus your agency's).
- A "no consent" concern means you must capture consent on the candidate before you can submit them.

## Tips

- The score weights skills most heavily (70%), then experience (20%) and location (10%) — so add good skills data to your candidates for sharper matches.
