## What this is for

Messages helps you write the routine notes recruiters send all day — requesting documents, inviting to interviews, sending shortlists, following up, polite rejections — quickly and consistently.

## Step-by-step

1. Open **Messages**.
2. Pick a template and (optionally) link a candidate to fill their name; add the role and any notes.
3. Select **Use template** for the standard wording, or **Draft with AI** to generate a tailored message.
4. Review and edit the subject and message.
5. **Send email** (server), **Open in WhatsApp**, or **Copy** to paste elsewhere.

## Rules

- Emails only send for real in production. In other environments the send is **simulated** so no candidate is contacted by accident.

## Tips

- Always read an AI draft before sending — treat it as a strong first draft, not a final word.
