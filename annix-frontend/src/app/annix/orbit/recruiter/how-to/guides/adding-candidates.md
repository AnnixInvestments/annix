## What this is for

Your candidates are the agency's core asset. Each candidate carries their skills, experience, **visibility** (who in the agency can see them) and **POPIA consent** (whether they may be submitted to a client).

## Step-by-step

1. Open **Candidates** and select **Add candidate**.
2. To save typing, use **Upload a CV to auto-fill** — Orbit reads the CV and pre-fills name, contact, skills and experience for you to review.
3. Set **visibility**:
   - *Private to me* — only you can see this candidate.
   - *My agency* — everyone in your agency can see them.
   - *Public* — only applies once the candidate has their own Seeker account and has consented.
4. Capture skills (comma-separated), availability, salary expectation and notes.
5. If the candidate has agreed their CV may be sent to clients, tick **consent to share** and record how consent was obtained.
6. Save.

## Rules

- A candidate **cannot be submitted to a client** until *consent to share* is ticked — this is a POPIA requirement, enforced by the system.
- Private candidates are hidden from other recruiters in your agency.
- The **Activity** panel on each candidate records every share and consent change.

## Tips

- Capture consent at the moment the candidate agrees — the timestamp and source are stored for your audit trail.
