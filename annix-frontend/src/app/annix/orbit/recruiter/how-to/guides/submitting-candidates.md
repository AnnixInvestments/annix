## What this is for

A submission is the record of a candidate being put forward to a client for a role, tracked from submitted through to placed or rejected.

## Step-by-step

1. Open **Submissions** and select **Submit candidate**.
2. Choose the candidate. **Only candidates who have consented to share appear in the list.**
3. Link the client and enter the job title.
4. Save. As the client responds, update the status (viewed → interested → interview → offer → placed / rejected).
5. Capture client feedback against the submission as it comes in.

## Rules

- The system **blocks** submitting a candidate without POPIA consent — you'll see a clear message telling you to capture consent first.
- Every submission is recorded in the candidate's Activity trail.

## Tips

- If a candidate you want isn't in the dropdown, open their profile and tick *consent to share* once they've agreed.
