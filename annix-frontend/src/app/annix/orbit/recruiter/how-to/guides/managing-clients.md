## What this is for

Clients are the companies you recruit for. Keeping them here gives every vacancy, submission and placement a home, and keeps fee terms and relationship notes in one place.

## Step-by-step

1. Open **Clients** from the sidebar.
2. Select **Add client**.
3. Capture the company name, industry and location.
4. Add the contact person, email and phone.
5. Record your **placement fee %** and **payment terms** so they're ready when you place.
6. Set the **relationship status** (prospect, active, on hold, inactive).
7. Save. Click any row later to edit.

## Rules

- Clients are visible to everyone in your agency.
- Deleting a client does not delete its placements or submissions, but you'll lose the linked contact details.

## Tips

- Use the notes field for things that save time later — "rejects job hoppers", "fast interview process", "slow payer".
