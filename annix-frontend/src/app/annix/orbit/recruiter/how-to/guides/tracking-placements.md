## What this is for

Placements are where you get paid. This page tracks each placement's fee, start date, guarantee period and invoice status, and totals your fees in pipeline.

## Step-by-step

1. Open **Placements** and select **Record placement**.
2. Enter the candidate name and job title, and link the client.
3. Capture the salary and your **placement fee**.
4. Set the **start date** and **guarantee until** date.
5. Track the status (offer accepted → started → in guarantee → completed, or fall-off) and the **invoice status** (not invoiced → invoiced → paid).

## Rules

- The **Fees in pipeline** total at the top sums the placement fees so you can see expected revenue at a glance.

## Tips

- Set the guarantee-until date so you know when a placement is safe and a replacement is no longer owed.
