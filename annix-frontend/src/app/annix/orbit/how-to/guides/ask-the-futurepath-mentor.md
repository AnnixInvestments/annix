## What the mentor is

The FuturePath mentor answers questions about study choices, universities and
funding. It reasons **only over your own profile and our curated data** — it does
not invent universities, entry requirements, bursaries or deadlines.

## Step-by-step

1. Fill in your education profile and academic results first — the mentor is only
   as good as the information it can see.
2. On the **FuturePath** page, scroll to **Ask the FuturePath mentor**.
3. Type your question, e.g. *"Which degrees fit my marks, and what funding could I
   apply for?"*
4. Click **Ask mentor** and read the grounded answer.

## What it is NOT

- It is **not a substitute for a school counsellor**. For high-stakes decisions,
  confirm with your counsellor or the institution directly.
- It will tell you when it doesn't have enough information rather than guess — if
  that happens, add the missing detail to your profile and ask again.

## Tip

Every answer is logged so we can keep the mentor honest and accountable. Ask
specific questions (mention subjects, a field of interest, or a country) for the
most useful guidance.
