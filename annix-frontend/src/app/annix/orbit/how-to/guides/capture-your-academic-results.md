## How it works

FuturePath evaluates your marks against each programme's published admission
requirements. The more complete your results, the more accurate your matches.

## Step-by-step

1. On the **FuturePath** page, find the **Academic results** card.
2. Type a **Subject** name (e.g. Mathematics, Physical Sciences, English).
3. Enter the **Mark %** you achieved (or expect to achieve).
4. Enter the **Year** the result is from.
5. Click **Add**. The result appears in your list.
6. Repeat for each subject. Use **Remove** to delete a result you entered by mistake.

## Tips

- Enter your real subject names — FuturePath recognises common subjects (e.g. it
  knows Mathematics is different from Mathematical Literacy, which some
  programmes require specifically).
- If you only have predicted marks, enter those for now and update them when your
  final results arrive.

## Rules

- Marks are percentages between 0 and 100.
- For learners under the consent age, a guardian must record consent before
  results can be processed (see the guardian-consent guide).
