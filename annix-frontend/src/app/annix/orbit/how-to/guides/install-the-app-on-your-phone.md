## What is it?

Annix Orbit runs in your web browser, but you can **add it to your phone's home
screen** so it behaves just like an app you'd download from the store: its own
icon, a full-screen view with no browser bar, and one tap to open. There's
nothing to install from the App Store or Play Store — it's free, instant, and
always up to date.

## Why bother?

- **One tap away** — no typing the web address every time.
- **Full screen** — more room for your CV, jobs and applications.
- **Feels like an app** — your own Annix icon sits next to your other apps.
- **Always current** — it updates itself; you never download a new version.

## On an iPhone or iPad (Safari)

1. Open Annix Orbit in **Safari** (this won't work in Chrome on iPhone).
2. Tap the **Share** button — the square with an arrow pointing up, at the
   bottom of the screen.
3. Scroll down and tap **Add to Home Screen**.
4. Check the name (you can shorten it), then tap **Add** in the top corner.
5. The Annix icon now sits on your home screen — tap it to open Orbit full screen.

## On an Android phone (Chrome)

1. Open Annix Orbit in **Chrome**.
2. Tap the **⋮ menu** (three dots) in the top-right corner.
3. Tap **Add to Home screen** (some phones show **Install app**).
4. Confirm by tapping **Add** or **Install**.
5. The Annix icon now appears with your other apps — tap it to open Orbit.

## Tips

- Sign in once after installing and Orbit will usually keep you signed in.
- If you don't see **Add to Home Screen**, make sure you're using **Safari** on
  iPhone or **Chrome** on Android — other browsers may hide the option.
- Already added it and the icon looks wrong? Remove it from the home screen and
  add it again to pick up the latest icon.
