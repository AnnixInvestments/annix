## What is FuturePath?

FuturePath is the education-to-funding part of Annix Orbit. It helps you work out
which university programmes you could get into based on your school marks, and
gives you grounded guidance from the FuturePath mentor. It is the first step
before Orbit's job-search tools later in your journey.

## Step-by-step

1. Open **FuturePath** from the seeker menu.
2. In the **Education profile** card, choose your **Curriculum** (e.g. NSC for the
   South African National Senior Certificate, or A-Level for the UK).
3. Enter your **Country** (2-letter code, e.g. ZA), **Nationality** and **School**.
4. Enter your **Date of birth**. This determines whether guardian consent is
   needed before your profile can be processed (see the guardian-consent guide).
5. Click **Save profile**.

## Why it matters

Your curriculum and country decide which scoring rules apply — every university
scores applicants differently, so an accurate profile gives you accurate
guidance. You can update your profile any time as your details change.
