## What is it?

On the **My CV & Documents** page you can add a qualification or certificate by
taking a photo with your phone camera — no need to find a scanner. Nix reads the
photo straight away and fills in the details (the credential name, who issued
it, and the year), so it can sharpen your job matches immediately.

This feature is available on the **Pathfinder** and **Trailblazer** plans.

## How it works

1. Open **My CV & Documents** from the menu.
2. Scroll to **Qualifications** or **Certificates**.
3. Under the normal upload button, tap **Take Photo**.
4. Point your camera at the document and capture it (or pick an existing photo
   from your gallery).
5. Wait a moment while Nix reads it — you'll see a progress popup. When it's
   done, the credential appears in your list with a **Phone photo** tag.

## Add a clear scan later

A phone photo is great for getting started, but it isn't shared with employers
on its own. The credential is marked **"Not shown to employers yet"** until you
upload a clear copy.

When you're near a scanner (or have a proper PDF), use the **Upload
qualification / certificate** button in the same section to add the clear copy.
That automatically replaces the photo as your employer-facing version and stops
the reminder emails.

## Reminders

If you haven't added a clear scan, Annix Orbit emails you a gentle reminder —
once every few days, a few times — until you do. You can ignore it; nothing is
deleted, and your photo keeps helping Nix match you to jobs in the meantime.

## Tips

- Lay the document flat and fill the frame for the clearest read.
- Good lighting helps Nix pick up the institution and dates.
- You can add as many qualifications and certificates as you like — one per
  photo.
